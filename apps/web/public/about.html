About Xygo

We build the operating system around how the business actually works.

Xygo is an AI systems company for businesses that have outgrown disconnected tools but do not fit neatly into a fixed SaaS workflow.

Xygo is not another app to configure.

Many SMBs already have a CRM, calendar, file storage, accounting software, email, spreadsheets, and a set of manual follow-ups. The problem is that those tools rarely become a coherent operating environment.

Xygo studies the operation first, then generates and deploys the system that ties the business together.

Blueprint-led

Every build starts with roles, workflows, data, documents, approvals, reports, and customer visibility.

Module-based

Reusable portals, dashboards, scheduling, document, reporting, and AI components are customized to the client.

Implementation-focused

The customer gets a finished operating environment, not a blank product with setup instructions.

AI with controls

AI agents are tied to real work and human approval, not vague chatbot promises.

Where We Start

Operationally complex service businesses.

Xygo starts with construction, field services, inspections, engineering, and home services because the work is high-context, document-heavy, and still coordinated through too many disconnected places.

Xygo creates custom AI-powered operating systems that help businesses unify workflows, tools, teams, documents, and operational intelligence.
