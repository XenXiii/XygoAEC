The Problem

AEC teams still manage critical decisions across fragmented systems and delayed handoffs.

Blueprint reviews live in PDFs, inboxes, spreadsheets, markup tools, and disconnected conversations.

That fragmentation creates operational blindness: incomplete packages go unnoticed, coordination issues surface late, and estimators inherit risk with weak context.

Xygo’s role is not to add another silo. It becomes the AI-native operating layer above the workflow.
