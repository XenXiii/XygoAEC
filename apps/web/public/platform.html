Generative Business Infrastructure

The blueprint for turning business workflows into operating systems.

Xygo starts with how a company actually works, maps the modules it needs, and deploys a branded operating system with portals, dashboards, automations, documents, scheduling, reporting, integrations, and workflow-specific AI agents.

Blueprint Engine

Xygo determines what to build before it builds anything.

The first layer is structured workflow understanding: business type, roles, jobs, customer touchpoints, data objects, documents, approvals, pain points, and service-tool integrations.

Business Intake

Capture industry, services, customer journey, internal handoffs, reporting requirements, and operational bottlenecks.

Workflow Map

Translate the business into repeatable flows such as lead intake, scheduling, field work, approval, invoice, and follow-up.

Generated Plan

Recommend modules, roles, dashboards, portal views, AI agents, integrations, and a staged implementation path.

Module Library

Reusable modules become custom business systems.

Front Office

CRM Intake & Scheduling

Lead capture, qualification, booking, calendar availability, reminders, dispatch, and follow-up automation.

Client Experience

Branded Client Portal

Project status, approved reports, uploads, messages, invoices, payment status, and customer-facing visibility.

Operations

Field Reporting

Photos, notes, checklists, voice notes, AI report drafts, human approval, and client delivery.

Risk

Compliance & Insurance Review

Permits, COIs, contract requirements, missing documents, expirations, and correction-request drafting.

Finance

Accounting Dashboard

Invoices, job costing, estimate versus actual, receivables, expense visibility, and future QuickBooks/Xero sync.

AI Workforce

Workflow Agents

AI receptionist, project coordinator, document reviewer, report writer, compliance manager, and sales assistant.
